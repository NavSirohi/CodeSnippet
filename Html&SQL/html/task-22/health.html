Chocolate Health Effects

  • Positive
    1. Cocoa or dark chocolate may positively affect the circulatory system.
    2. Chocolate may boost cognitive abilities.
    3. Dark chocolate may lower cholesterol levels.
    4. Chocolate consumption may reduce Body Mass Index.
    5. Chocolate may have antioxidant and brain-stimulating effects.
  • Negative
    1. Chocolate may cause heartburn.
    2. Excess consumption can lead to obesity.
    3. Chocolate contains high calories due to fats and sugar.
    4. Chocolate contains oxalates that may increase kidney stone risk.
    5. Some chocolates may contain traces of lead.
